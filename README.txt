Drum Kit

Developed a drum kit where users can play different drum using clicking the mouse or pressing keys.

Motivation

Developed it while completing the course "The Complete 2022 Web Development Bootcamp". Aim was to learn the use of event handlers and audio method in JavaScript.

Tech/Framework used

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Tests

Click on the image button or press the specific key to play the sound.
